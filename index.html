May all beings find happiness

Many teachings of the Buddha emphasize the importance of developing genuine lovingkindness and compassion towards everyone, without discrimation.

For example, In The Jewel Ornament Of Liberation, Gampopa writes:
[O]ne should practice developing a pure desire for all sentient beings, limitless as space, to have happiness and benefit.
Bodhi Path Santa Barbara endeavors to create a safe and welcoming space for all people who wish to deepen their spiritual journey inclusive of all genders, races, sexual identities, ages, ethnicities, and other characteristics.
